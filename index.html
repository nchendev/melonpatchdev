FAQ

What is MelonPatch?

+

MelonPatch is an indie software studio that builds AI-powered tools and experiences. Founded in 2026, the studio's philosophy is to ship small, iterate fast, and care deeply about the people who use their products. We focus on AI-powered tools, creative software, and indie games.

What is the MelonPatch AI eBook Reader?

+

The MelonPatch AI eBook Reader is an iOS app built for language learners. Users can upload ebooks (TXT or EPUB) in any language and read with AI-powered tap-to-translate, context-aware phrase detection, native pronunciation, and built-in spaced-repetition flashcard review. Words and phrases can be bookmarked to a word bank and reviewed with flashcard decks organized by language.

What file formats does the eBook Reader support?

+

The AI eBook Reader supports TXT and EPUB file formats. You can upload books in any language and set any target "native language" for translations.

How does the flashcard review work?

+

While reading, tap any word or phrase to translate it. Bookmark the translation and it gets added to your word bank. The app creates separate flashcard decks for each language you're learning. Review uses spaced repetition to help you retain vocabulary naturally over time.

When will the AI eBook Reader be available?

+

The AI eBook Reader is coming soon to the iOS App Store. Follow us on Discord or reach out via email to stay updated on the launch.
