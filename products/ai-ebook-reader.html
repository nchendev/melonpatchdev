The MelonPatch AI eBook Reader is an iOS application for language learners. It supports TXT and EPUB file formats. Users can upload books in any language and use AI-powered tap-to-translate for context-aware translations, hear native pronunciation, and save words to a word bank. The app includes spaced-repetition flashcard review with separate decks per language.

Features

T

Tap-to-Translate

Tap any word or phrase while reading for an instant, context-aware translation powered by AI.

C

Context-Aware Phrases

AI detects multi-word phrases and idioms, providing translations that account for the surrounding context.

P

Pronunciation Guides

Hear native pronunciation for any word or phrase. Learn how it actually sounds, not just what it means.

W

Word Bank

Bookmark translations while reading. Words and phrases are saved to your personal word bank, organized by language.

F

Flashcard Review

Built-in spaced-repetition flashcards with separate decks per language. Retain vocabulary naturally over time.

B

Any Book, Any Language

Upload TXT or EPUB files in any language. Set your native language and start reading — no restrictions.

How It Works

1

Upload Your Book

Import any TXT or EPUB file. Read books in Japanese, Spanish, French, Korean, Mandarin — any language you're learning.

2

Read and Translate

Tap any word or phrase for an instant, context-aware translation. The AI understands the surrounding text to give you accurate meanings.

3

Save and Review

Bookmark words to your word bank. Review them later with spaced-repetition flashcards that help you retain vocabulary over time.
